Behringer: Poly D

Behringer has announced the Poly D, a four-voice paraphonic clone of the Minimoog, with a 37 key, velocity-sensitive keyboard. Poly D features a pure analogue signal path, based on Moog's original Model D circuit, with matched transistors.
